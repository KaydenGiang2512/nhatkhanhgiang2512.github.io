MY PROJECT IDEA

view feedbacks

OVERVIEW

  As a quick summarization, my project idea is to program and develop an app for mobile devices designated for health monitoring and fitness tracking. In all honesty, this idea is relatively popular in the modern world we are currently in, especially with the mainstream of mass media and the process of globalization making public awareness of health ever so high, which means that my idea is not completely original. Having said that, I have always wanted to attempt this innovation myself and create my own version of a fitness app that supervises your physical conditions and inform its users of potential health-related risks based on the input symptoms and generate solutions to prevent such problems. Also, this app will entail a function which allows users to give feedback of my product for future improvements, such as the app performance itself, how useful is the information provided and the precision of the medical procedures within the app.

MOTIVATION

  Since I was a young child, my family has always emphasized the essentiality of adequate health and how regular exercise can maintain peak conditions and prevent most illnesses. Consequently, I am highly aware of my physical and mental well-being and I always try to lead a healthy, sensible and balanced lifestyle as much as I possibly can. My biggest reason for taking up this ambitious project is the fact that humanity is undergoing one of the worst pandemics ever recorded in recent history: the COVID 19 plague. This viral catastrophe is spreading at an unprecedented pace, claiming over one million lives after a single year and has infected millions. 

  Moreover, 2020 has witnessed many tragic losses with celebrities untimely dying due to abysmal causes, such as Kobe Bryant, Chadwick Boseman, Diego Maradona, and most recently famous Vietnamese singer and comedian Chi Tai. These heartbreaking events shook the world and as a result raised the importance of good healthcare to another level, and that was the true inspiration I needed to make my decision on developing a convenient, inexpensive and practical method to examine your health.

DESCRIPTION

  Essentially, this application is not that much different from a standard personal healthcare app in terms of functionalities and interface. As a frequent user myself, I regret to say that my overall experience with fitness apps have not been entirely satisfactory, since there were several aspects that I felt could be greatly improved on. Additionally, I have experienced several apps of this particular type for quite some time and I did notice a few common drawbacks and similar patterns in all of them.     

  Firstly, the questions are usually quite repetitive, illogical to some extent and they simply target the general demographic as a single measurement to make unspecific assumptions and eventually come to conclusions, which can possibly lead to misinterpretation for patients and detrimental mistakes. As a consequence, these results can only serve as a relatively acceptable reference and is not really reliable. This is especially critical for patients who are suffering from rare illnesses or is a special case that goes beyond the scope of those rigid categories. Another visible flaw is in the diagnostics of these apps. Again, they lack accuracy and would oftentimes show you the worst-case scenario, with little to no information on how to avoid or prevent imminent threats to your future health. Lastly, I was not very fond of the complicated design that some of those apps had. Though it is undeniable that it enhanced the aesthetics of the final product, an overly decorated application can lead to confusion and annoyance.     

  For the reasons mentioned above, my project will be created with the intention of enhancing the field of healthcare apps and fixing those imperfections previously stated to ensure satisfaction for my customers. Some of its best features will include user personalization, more precise diagnosis, user-friendly interface, thorough health examinations and evaluations, informative suggestions/tips for preserving and improving health conditions, constant updates to maintain excellent user experience and so on. Furthermore, the database of this app will be directly linked to hospitals to monitor patients remotely and effectively enhance doctor-patient communication (with user confidentiality and encryption), for rapid responses and life-saving situations, and also to save time on tedious processes like preliminary diagnosis, patient introduction and basic field tests.      

  To be more specific, this project encompasses the typical procedures that you would go through during a periodical check-up, such as registering your personal information (Name, email, national ID, phone number, D.O.B, gender, home address), a list of your medical history, list of allergies and hereditary syndromes/diseases (if any), inputting your current symptoms/illnesses in a text box, receiving the corresponding diagnostics in color-coded format (Organized by the level of severity) and followed up by recommendations for potential treatment/prevention with detailed guidelines when clicked on. Furthermore, this apps provides users with various options to improve their daily habits, diet patterns, workout routines based on their individual needs and their current state of health (obesity avoidance, increase muscle mass, cardio, calorie burn, stamina).   
 
   As a disclaimer, this healthcare app I am planning to create is by no means a permanent replacement or substitute for actual doctors as well as health institutions, simply because apps do not have the ability to cure illnesses the same way that medical experts can. Instead, think of it as a free, easily accessible consultation service for patients to keep track of their health conditions, be notified of potential risks towards their well-being and avert them and learn about methods to sustain a balanced life with longevity.

TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES

  In order to construct this mobile application, there are quite a few factors that come into play. For software, I will primarily be using coding apps such as Pycharm Pro and VS code to program this app, in conjunction to some free online app builders (Oracle Apex, MIT App Inventor, Firebase) to simplify the procedure. It is also worth mentioning that Python will be the main programming language I am using because of its ubiquity in the IT world and uncomplicated syntax, and due to the fact that I am currently learning Python at RMIT. If necessary, I will also implement free tools to aid my app developing process like Marvel or Invision because they allow you to make apps for both the App Store and Google Play Store. When it comes to hardware, a single laptop (preferably windows 10, core i5 7th gen or later) that has sufficient processing power to handle multiple tasks simultaneously would be more than enough for me to attempt this project, because constructing an app is not too reliant on hardware to begin with.  

SKILLS REQUIRED

  Over the span of three months since my enrollment in RMIT, I managed to acquire quite a few skills and techniques that are particularly useful for conducting this project such as productive research, writing professional technical reports, solving problems by applying the “divide & conquer” approach, understanding of some basic coding concepts and most importantly key principles for effective groupwork. Apart from the set of skills I currently have, there are so much more invaluable knowledge that I could learn in the future to expand my field of expertise.        

  In order to do so, I need to consult my professors and lecturers at RMIT so that they can give me advice on how I should tackle this idea, as well as doing extensive research on the topic by myself.  Fortunately, the technology I will be implementing into my project is readily available all over the Internet in terms of hardware and software, so feasibility is reasonably high and work can begin immediately without much complications. Those are most of the requirements I could come up with for the time being, and I am certain that future changes will be made to suit my given circumstances and to ensure the success of this app.

OUTCOME

  Since my app is designated for monitoring health and enhancing the quality of personal healthcare, the pinnacle of success for this project would be to see this app carry out its desirable operations flawlessly without any issues or errors what so ever. My highest expectations are nothing more than a fully-functional healthcare application that can accurately evaluate most common illnesses and health risks based on the given symptoms from patients, then perform actual diagnostics of the seriousness of their conditions and finally design an effective treatment plan with intensive guidelines to alleviate the situation. Moreover, I want my potential users to have the ability to send me feedback of the service quality they received, and from there give suggestions/recommendations on how to make their experience with my service even more enjoyable. Despite this app not being a game-changing innovation nor is it a cutting-edge piece of technology, I am confident that it still has a significant influence on our society as a whole and will hopefully change people’s perception of free healthcare services in a positive way.
